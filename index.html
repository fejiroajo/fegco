Frequently Asked Questions

...Can't find an answer? Ask us today!

  • What is Fegco Health?

    Fegco Health is Emergency response company that helps connect people with emergency needs to appropriate emergency responders such as ground private ambulance, hospital ambulance and air ambulance.

  • What is the website for?

    The website is to make it easy for those who need fast response during emergency such as an accident or heart attack etc to connect to different emergencies .

  • Can I access a nearby hospital in real-time?

    Yes. The website allows you to enter your location and then the google map will immediately bring up the operational hospital nearby which you can then choose your preference.

  • Is your service expensive?

    Our services are relatively cheap for hospitals, ambulances and free for members.

  • Where is your office located?

    We have offices in different countries in Africa but our Headquarter is in Accra, Ghana along Winter Street.
